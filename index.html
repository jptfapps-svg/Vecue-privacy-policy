Privacy Policy for Vecue

Effective Date: 04/09/2025
Last Updated: 04/09/2025
Applies to: Android iOS Windows

1. Introduction

Welcome to Vecue ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our vehicle maintenance tracking application (the "App") across all supported platforms: Android, iOS, and Windows.

Vecue is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting or storing your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes our data practices and those of third-party services that may be integrated with the App.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

Vecue does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to our servers. This includes:

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Vehicle information (make, model, registration, VIN)
  • ❌ Service history and maintenance records
  • ❌ Cost and expense data
  • ❌ Location information
  • ❌ Usage patterns or behavior data
  • ❌ Any data you enter into the app

3. How Your Data is Stored

3.1 Local Storage Only

  • All your data remains on your device - Vehicle profiles, maintenance records, costs, and all other information you enter is stored locally on your device
  • No server uploads - We do not upload, sync, or backup your data to external servers
  • Your control - You have complete control over your data since it never leaves your device

Platform-Specific Storage Locations:

  • Windows: %APPDATA%\Vecue\ or %LOCALAPPDATA%\Vecue\
  • Android: App's private internal storage directory
  • iOS: App's sandbox Documents directory

3.2 Data Sync Feature (Full Version)

  • Future feature - Data synchronization across devices is planned for the Full Version but is currently in development
  • Your choice - When available, data sync will be optional and require your explicit consent
  • Transparency - We will update this policy before implementing any data sync features

5. Permissions We May Request

The app may request the following permissions for functionality, depending on your platform:

5.1 Storage/File Access

  • Purpose: To save your vehicle data and maintenance records locally on your device
  • Data access: Only data you manually enter into the app
  • Platform notes:
    • Android Storage permission for local file access
    • iOS No explicit permission required (sandboxed storage)
    • Windows File system access for local storage

5.2 Notifications

  • Purpose: To show notifications for upcoming maintenance reminders
  • Data access: None - notifications are generated locally based on your maintenance schedule
  • Availability: Full Version feature when available

5.3 Camera Access

  • Purpose: To capture photos of receipts or vehicle documents for your records
  • Data access: Photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Platform notes: Available on all platforms if applicable

5.4 Network Access

  • Purpose: Only for checking app updates or license validation (if applicable)
  • Data transmitted: No personal data - only app version and basic system information

Note: All permissions are optional and the app will function without them, though some features may be limited. You can manage these permissions through your device's settings.

6. Data Security

6.1 Local Security

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device lock screens, encryption, and security features provided by your operating system
  • Regular app updates include security improvements
  • Data is stored with appropriate file permissions on each platform

6.2 No Network Transmission

  • Since we don't transmit your data over networks, there's no risk of data interception during transmission
  • Your sensitive vehicle and maintenance information never leaves your device

7. Your Data Rights

7.1 Complete Control

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: Edit or update any information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual records or all data through the app settings
  • Export: Export your data for backup purposes (Full Version feature when available)

7.2 Data Portability

  • Your data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • You can manually backup your device to preserve your Vecue data
  • Full version may include export and import functionality for easier data portability

8. Data Retention

  • Your control: Data is retained only as long as you keep it in the app
  • Manual deletion: You can delete any or all data at any time through the app
  • App removal: Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device
  • No server storage: Since we don't store data on servers, there's no server-side data to retain or delete

9. Platform-Specific Considerations

Android Android-Specific

  • Google Play Services may collect app performance data
  • Android system may collect usage statistics based on your privacy settings
  • App data is stored in the app's private directory, protected by Android's permission system

iOS iOS-Specific

  • App data is stored within the app's sandbox, protected by iOS security
  • iOS may collect app analytics based on your privacy settings
  • All data remains within the app's secure container

Windows Windows-Specific

  • Windows Update may collect information about installed applications
  • Your antivirus software may scan Vecue files as part of system protection
  • The app may trigger User Account Control (UAC) prompts for certain operations

10. Children's Privacy

Since Vecue doesn't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's data. However, the app is designed for vehicle owners and is most suitable for users 16 and older.
